Raising Labradoodles

My husband, Dr. Mark Medldleton, DVM and I, Becky Meddleton, take great pleasure in raising our labradoodles. We are located near Albuquerque, New Mexico, often called the Land of Enchantment. We consider Labradoodles to be the dogs of Enchantment. They have gentle and kind spirits mixed with just the right amount of playfulness. They are smart, social, loyal, and easy to train. Further, their soft wavy coats and athletic bodies give them an uncommonly beautiful appearance that speaks of casual elegance. And, of course, their low to non-shedding, allergy friendly coats help make Labradoodles a pleasure to have in your home

Our
Pledge

As a veterinarian, Mark is particularly focused on breeding the finest Australian Labradoodle puppies with temperament, health and sound structure being the priorities. We do extensive evaluations and testing on our breeding stock to ensure that we are breeding top quality dogs. We breed standard and medium size Labradoodles in various colors of cream, apricot, red, caramel, chocolate and black. We utilize current research to raise them in a way that promotes health and trainability. Structure, temperament , and health are the guiding principles of our breeding program.
