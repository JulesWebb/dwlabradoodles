Breeding Labradoodle: Liberty

Desert Waves Statue at Liberty, "Liberty"

We named this beautiful girl "Statue at Liberty", because she has such stunning structure that she literally looks like a statue in motion. We love her structure, the shape of her head, her big round sparkling chestnut eyes – everything about her. She comes from our favorite lines which are known for producing the most rock solid temperaments in the labradoodle breed. A number of service dogs have come from these lines. She is intelligent, eager to learn and very friendly. We are excited about the outstanding Multigeneration Australian Labradoodle puppies she will be producing for us.

Liberty's Info

  • ALAA Registration No: ALAA-026641
  • Mom: Hale's Rosalita
  • Dad: Hale's Chocolate Chiago
  • Color: Chocolate
  • Coat: Curly Fleece
  • Size: Standard - 21" at the shoulder, 38 lbs

Health Clearances

  • Elbows - normal
  • CERF - no inhertited eye disease found
  • Thyroid levels - normal
  • Patella luxation - normal
  • Cardiac Exam - Normal
  • vWD - clear by parentage
