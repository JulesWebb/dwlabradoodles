Policies & Fees

Excellence in Breeding

It takes effort, care and money to produce puppies of the good health, quality and temperament that we have here at Desert Waves Labradoodles. We put time and attention to careful selection of breeding stock. Temperament, health, and coat quality are a priority for us. It is our hope that you will find your dog well worth the price!

Meeting Your New Family Member

We schedule appointments for each waiting family to come for Puppy Selection at about 6 weeks of age when the puppies are old enough for us to make good matches between puppies and families. We can do this via email and teleconference if necessary, but families should plan to come either for the Selection Day or to pick up their puppy on the Go-Home day (or both!). We value the opportunity to sit down with you and go over any questions you might have about puppy care and your new labradoodle family member.

Policies

We endeavor to breed genetically sound dogs. The genetic testing of our breeding dogs includes:

  • Hip clearances by PennHip method or OFA
  • OFA Elbows
  • CERF eye exam
  • PRA (prcd) which can be cleared by parentage
  • And other health tests such as Thyroid and CBC panels

Your Desert Waves labradoodle puppy comes with the following:

  1. Spay or Neuter Surgery
  2. 30 Days of Free Pet Medical Insurance through trupanion
  3. Current Vaccinations and Deworming
  4. Microchip
  5. Pedigree
  6. 2 Year Genetic Health Warranty (see our contract for details)
  7. Puppy Collar and Leash
  8. All Medical Records

Fees

A Desert Waves Australian Labradoodle is: $2,500.00

  • We will ship your dog (if required) at buyer's expense.
  • Puppies are available to go home at 8 weeks of age. If you cannot take your puppy at that time, you will be charged $10.00 per day for care and feeding of your puppy. (If you require shipping, this fee will be applied until your puppy is able to be shipped safely per reliable carrier's requirements. )
